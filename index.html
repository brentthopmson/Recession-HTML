How to apply and receive your Recession Assistance Payments

The stages of receiving your Recession Assistance Funds for business, self-employed individuals, or independent contractors are described below:

  • Complete your Preliminary Application
  • Receive your secure QR Barcode
  • Contact your appointed Finance Officer (Training on how to complete your recession assistance deposit at the Digital Currency Machine and mobile dispatch application for the first time)
  • Receive your first payment in your bank account and complete your first dispatch duty immediately using your QR barcode.

After completing your first dispatch duty, you will be experienced in completing your future duties with the assistance of your Finance Officer.

JOIN US TODAY

To qualify for the Recession Assistance Funds for citizens of the United States of America, you must:

  • Live in the US or its territories
  • Be available for manual verification of identification and banking information, and completion of dispatch duties at every payment cycle.
  • Operate a highly credible bank account where your recession assistance funds will be paid recurrently. If you do not have a bank account, you may request financial advice from the Finance Desk on the best financial institution to open an account within your community.
  • Be available during a recession assistance dispatch cycle to fund the Digital Currency Machine closest to your home address. Performance during each cycle will influence improvement in your personal and business credit scores to GOOD/EXCELLENT within one calendar year.

Providing financial assistance to those struggling during economic downturns. PAP is committed to supporting individuals and businesses through recession relief payments.

For Physical Machine Dispatch (Bitcoin ATM):

Once payment is available in your bank account, you will be instructed regarding the funds splitting details by your appointed Finance Officer.

After separating your personal benefits amount, withdraw cash and go to the Digital Currency Machine (Bitcoin ATM) location with the cash amount of recession assistance funds instructed by your FO. Verify your identity physically on the machine (Phone number and Picture verification) and make your dispatch deposit on the machine using your QR Bar-code.

After the successful dispatch, forward a clear image of your dispatch receipt generated from the automated teller machine to the Finance Desk and your appointed Finance Officer for validation and preparation for your next dispatch.

For Mobile Dispatch Application (Blockchain, Coinbase & Trust Wallet):

Once payment is available in your bank account, you will be instructed regarding the funds splitting details by your appointed Finance Officer.

After separating your personal benefits amount from the received payment, transfer the recession assistance amount into the mobile dispatch application you are registered and verified on. Your personal information must be the same as your wallet information to avoid fraud act triggers, which may lead to termination of your assistance program.

When the Recession Assistance payment is available in your secure wallet, carefully complete your mobile dispatch using your QR Bar-code or wallet address. It is mandatory you are guided by your appointed Finance Officer while completing your first mobile dispatch.

Your main duty, representing yourself or your business will be completing your recession assistance dispatch deposit at the verified Digital Currency (Bitcoin ATM) or mobile dispatch application provided by your appointed Finance Officer. For every successful recession assistance dispatch your personal and business credit score will be boosted, improving your financial history and giving you more financial freedom. All expenses during your recession assistance dispatch will be covered by Fintech Global (Included in your recession assistance payment amount).
